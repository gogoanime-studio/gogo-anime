Gainax

This is the studio that produced one of the most classic anime series of all time "Neon Genis Evangelion".

Production of I.G

Production IG is known for its huge number of TV series, OVAs, and movies, as well as its involvement in game graphic design, such as the Playstation 2 game Ghost in the Shell : Stand Alone Complex and Blood: The Last Vampire.

I.G's name is taken from the initials in the names of the company's two founders, Mitsuhisa Ishikawa and Takayuki Goto. Production I.G is best known for producing the anime series Ghost in the Shell, Psycho-Pass, Guilty Crown and Eden of the East. In 2013, Production I.G began adapting the first three parts of the popular manga Attack on Titan into an anime.

Animation Toei

Formerly known as Nihon Doga Eiga and Toei Doga, Toei Animation is an animation studio whose largest shareholder is the Toei company. During its development, the studio has released a lot of episodes, movies and movie adaptations that have resonated around the world. Hayao Miyazaki and Leiji Matsumoto used to work at Toei.

It is also Toei who has trained many world famous manga artists, some of whom are Eiichiro Oda, the author of One Piece - one of the beloved anime series across many systems. Toei also launched anime series such as GeGeGe no Kitaro, Sailor Moon, Slam Dunk, Yu-Gi-Oh, Digimon, and World Trigger.

Studio Pierrot

Studio Pierrot is an anime producer founded in 1979 with headquarters located in Mitaka, Tokyo. The studio's name is named after a classic human "Pierrot", which means "clown" in Japanese. Their logo also has a black and white clown face on it.

Studio Pierrot is a producer known mainly for such masterpieces as Naruto, Bleach, Tokyo Ghoul, Yu Yu Hakusho and Fushigi Yugi. Among them, Yu Yu Hakusho and Saiyuki were awarded the Animage Grand Prix. Until now Pierrot's works have been extremely profitable and profitable and show no signs of financial layoffs. This is also the main studio producing the works of Shounen Jump.

Sunrise is known as the producer behind the successful Cyborg 009 anime series Gundam, InuYasha, Gintama, Cowboy Bebop and Code Geass.
